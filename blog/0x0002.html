Professional or Passionate

2026-06-19

I'm thought about myself a lot in the past that when I have duty that must do, e.g., homework, exams, project group, and anything to be achieve the goal, I have the second thought that the brain told me to do something that use lower energy like doomscrolling social media, watch youtube, etc. and figure out that I'm using time so much and when I go back to work, I can't focused on work and I'm going back to social media again. The problem I think we are already knows that the problem come from huge dopamine that makes brain feel more exciting. Most people, even me, have a trapped on instant gratification, social media algorithm that designed to increase time usage, leads to lose focused on deep work. Instant isn't always bad, but it's bad if we let the algorithm control lives.

Even now we have AI (AI in this context refered to Generative AI, AI Agents, anything that use LLM) to help us have better experience on learning to make time for understanding shorter which is benefit, but it has side effect from who let AI do for avoid responsibilities is to replace themself thoughts. The side effect is to not improve brain cells because they let AI thought instead of themselves.

But the problem that I told are not actually the root cause. The root cause are various depends on the experience of each people, e.g., low self-esteem, fear of failure, high expectation, anything that cause to be addicted instant gratification for long time. The best examples are 1) on my first paragraph 2) self-study because of wanting achieve career which is look good, but feel like have no direction 3) fear of negative evaluation and criticism. Those example occur, brain think that have some damange, so it find a way to escape the reality into instant gratification immediately.

Normally, the nature of human always of the problem like this, e.g., failure, success, enjoy, bored, sad, happy, selfish, kindness, etc. Those problem are the human thing that AI don't even have because AI cannot think for themselves. AI look like think when answer to us, actually it is very huge mathematics represented to neural network. The thought process actually in data center that use huge of GPU cores, running LLM on around billion to trillion parameter which is very massive of resources. But to fix with those problem, there is no way to fully fix that problem immediately, so we need to be emotional management when we confront bad emotion sometimes. The best way to make emotional management is to find time to be alone and review our own thought, or let it be and find the hobby or our loves to do, e.g., play some video games, read books, some educational video, listening to music, play instrument, etc. Noted that I am not psychology, so if you have a huge problem on mental health, do your own research, and go to psychologist if it's severe.

Passionate

Passionate is about interesting in doing something that is valuable, e.g., make art, calligraphy, typing skills, even gaming. It doesn't meant that valuable to society, job description, transcript, salary, etc. It is valuable to inner of humanity. Passionate doesn't meant to show off to other ppl knows, doesn't meant to be afraid of falling behind, and doesn't meant to be professional career (but can). The thing is passion is temporary and always be, and this reason why I figured out to separate the passionate and professional because inner of everyone are not always happy, easy, perfect, unlike outer viewer.

In the age of AI era that self improvement are over on the internet, most content of self improvement are the same and refer to the same books, e.g., Atomic Habits, Rich Dad Poor Dad, etc. In marketing, these contents are sales feeling good which leads to dopamine hit to viewer think that they are improving themselves but actually not. I'm not telling you that all self improvement is bad, I like some videos that represented good personality and expression even though it might be making up, but must think about behind the scene, observe the storytelling, and always don't trust everything without actually doing to be actually improve about happiness, valueable, mental health, exercises, etc. I also watching Dr.K from HealthyGamerGG, exercise club, but don't like "You must wake up at 5AM, You must do this way to improve to top 1%, 99% still do this" or anything that feel like you are behind... Beware watching too much self improvement, especially without critical thinking, have side effect on having illusion on fake passion without actually doing, or the passion with the survival, that isn't gonna be happy in the long run

Even though self improvement content maybe or maybe not to have the good passion, only have passion without responsibilities or discipline are not reach to be professional, or get any achievement, and vice versa. E.g., CEOs of big tech enjoys the programming, do tech project before being CEO, but don't like being CEO, instead of left being CEO, they saw opportunity to be CEO and saw as duty. The first experience of the most ppl are literally doing homework and exams finish first, then allowed to play video games, or anything entertainments, that is the duty that you must do it to get achievements.

Professional

Only passionate without discipline cannot be professional, but doesn't meant to compel yourself to be professional and it not gotta work with negative push. Everyone must to overcoming the boredom from repetitive jobs even though jobs, or faculty of you learn are very passionate, e.g, report paper, 9-5 jobs.

To be professional, we must separate emotion, your problem, or personal out of the duty. Not only manners, but your personal values are also important to keeps you from feeling worse at work. To be making mistake, customer rude, even unemployed after graduated doesn't define your value. In the past, I was good at math in primary, then I was bad at math in secondary and I was thinking that I won't good at math anymore, then in high school I was good at math again. From this stories, to be bad at math in secondary doesn't define me that I won't good at math anymore, that is not true! but to be good at math again I must do self study session to find the pattern, formula, and questioning that is formula come from, why is those variable appear in equations.

AI makes rapidly professional?

I'm just like other person that I love to use AI as a tool for help to do research, explaining each topic to more understanding, write code that repetitive patterns, or anything, but I'm disagree to see AI as a brain to offload human thinking, e.g., vibe coding without knowing everything, and tell other ppl we don't need to know about programming to do it, or something like we are in apocalypse. I will ask you that if AI are completely replace developers (I know it won't happened anyway), then that is the saddest thing in history that many apps, or website are look very the same, because in reality AI have no brain! and the habit that I wrote (use AI for long way or be trapped in) are the thing that make you less professional and creativity!

From the above paragraph, AI actually can speed up learning if we see AI as a tools, teacher, searching, or even help repetitive like search reference or something that we don't know what you don't know to become know what you don't know to get more new perspective, find the statistic reliable source, write the pattern code, ask the syntax and use more time to focus on business logic. Yep, just change our mind from "Oh we don't need to know about this" to "How behind the scene works, why? read the source, or even recommend the books".

If you're a student and you're using AI to do your work, this is precisely the kind of job that will be replaced.
